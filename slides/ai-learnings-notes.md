# Copilot/AI Learnings


- Fix pitfalls rather than teaching agents to avoid them

---

- **Your dev environment IS the prompt.** If your config is broken, the AI will struggle too. Fix the root cause.
- AGENTS.md: less is more. The agent can discover most things itself. Only document what it can't reasonably find or keeps getting wrong.
- AGENTS.md isn't very composable
- Parallel subagents: delegate research, analysis, and reviews to multiple agents simultaneously.
- "Never let a mistake go to waste" — update memory/instructions when something goes wrong, but verify the lesson is correct.
- askQuestions tool: lets the agent ask clarifying questions instead of guessing.
